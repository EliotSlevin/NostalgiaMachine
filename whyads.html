Why are there ads?

To ensure we can run The Nostalgia Machine long term, it has to have some sort of income. We just don’t have the time or energy to support it solo, so ads are the best long term solution. We’re aiming for as minimal amount of ads as possible.

Wait I hate ads!

If you want an ad free experience on The Nostalgia Machine (and other sites), consider using an ad blocking plugin. These plugins prevent ads from being downloaded, hiding them from your view on many sites on the internet. We won’t hold it against you.

We can personally suggest AdBlock Plus, and µBlock.
